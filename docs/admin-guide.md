# How to Use the FinancialEDGE Admin Dashboard

## Logging In

1. Go to `https://financialedge.com.ng/admin`
2. Enter your username and password
3. Click "Sign In"

You will see the Dashboard with your website statistics.

---

## Writing a New Article

1. Click **Posts** in the left menu
2. Click the **"New Post"** button
3. Fill out the form:
   - **Title:** What your article is about
   - **Content:** Write your article here
   - **Category:** Pick where it belongs (Money Market, Energy, etc.)
   - **Featured Image:** Paste a link to your main image
   - **Excerpt:** A short summary (2-3 sentences)
4. Choose **Status**:
   - **Draft** = Save for later (not live yet)
   - **Published** = Go live immediately
5. Click **Publish** or **Save Draft**

---

## Editing an Article

1. Click **Posts** in the menu
2. Find your article in the list
3. Click **Edit**
4. Make your changes
5. Click **Update**

---

## Deleting an Article

1. Find the article in the Posts list
2. Click **Delete**
3. Click **Yes** to confirm

⚠️ **Warning:** Deleted articles cannot be recovered!

---

## Creating a Page (Like About Us, Contact)

Pages are for permanent content that doesn't change often.

1. Click **Pages**
2. Click **"New Page"**
3. Add title and content
4. Click **Publish**

---

## Adding Categories

Categories help organize your articles.

1. Click **Categories**
2. Click **"New Category"**
3. Enter:
   - **Name:** What readers see (e.g., "Stock Market")
   - **Slug:** The web address (e.g., "stock-market")
4. Click **Save**

---

## Uploading Pictures

1. Click **Media**
2. Click **"Upload"**
3. Select pictures from your computer
4. Add a description so Google can find them
5. Copy the link to use in your articles

---

## Website Settings

### Change Site Title and Description

1. Click **Settings** → **SEO**
2. Edit:
   - **Site Title:** Your website name
   - **Description:** What your site is about
3. Click **Save**

This helps Google show your site in search results.

---

## Quick Tips

✅ **Good Article Checklist:**
- Clear, catchy title
- Interesting picture
- Short summary (excerpt)
- Assigned to a category
- 3-5 tags that describe the topic

✅ **Pictures:**
- Use JPG or PNG files
- Keep under 1MB for fast loading
- Always add a description

✅ **Publishing:**
- Use **Draft** when writing
- Use **Published** when ready for readers

---

## Need Help?

**Problem:** Can't save my article  
**Fix:** Make sure you filled in the Title

**Problem:** Picture not showing  
**Fix:** Check the picture link is correct

**Problem:** Changes not appearing on website  
**Fix:** Wait 2 minutes, then refresh the page

**Problem:** Can't log in  
**Fix:** Contact the admin to reset your password
